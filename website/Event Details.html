Seating: General Admission seating means sitting on the ground. (most of Riverstage is GRASSED AREA on a gentle slope)

Bad Weather Policy:

Riverstage is an outdoor amphitheatre. If rain or bad weather occurs, the show will proceed, unless deemed unsafe to do so. No refunds will be authorised unless the event is cancelled

Resale:

Resale Tickets may be available for this event. These are tickets sold by fans and are verified by Ticketmaster.
